My Time In Model United Nations

It has been a busy, fun, best three days. I got in Addis Ababa International Community School Model United Nations. It was a debating program about military drones and civilian causalities. All the delegates were confident, smart and enthusiastic. Especially the delegate of North Korea defended his country bravely. He opposed every allegations non his country and won every debate he had. He inspired me to be confident and speak up. And I will always be grateful for that.

A second time I went to Model United Nations, it was at Saint Joseph School. This was also a great time which helped me to be more confident and believe in myself.
