What I'm Doing Now

I'm a second year student at Addis Ababa Institute of Techenology. I'm studying Software Engineering. I enjoy coding. It is like being able to create something.

I started coding in highschool junior year. I used to have friends that was seniors and they introduced me to coding.

I'm nineteen years old.I spend most of my time on school work and projects but, in my spare time I like sports, movies, books and music.

Untill this moment, I have worked with different programming and markup languages such as Python, Java, c++, HTML, XML, CSS.

I am also graduating from gebeya with Data Science learning track. The first time I heard about Gebeya was from a friend. When Gebeya announces the Microsoft 4 Africa skill lab program, I was already thinking of applying to women intake scholarship. What attracted me most to Microsoft 4 africa skilllab was that it has my interest as a learning track and also it gives opportunity to be Microsoft certified in different courses in one learning track. So I decided to join and I learned implementation of Data Science using Microsoft Azure.

My experience at Gebeya was more than I expected it was great. They gave us courses not only to get us certified but also for us to implement our knowledge. We also took soft skill courses to develop our communication and problem solving skills.

The financial and educational support from gebeya was very helpful and important since we had to conduct online classes due to lockdown. After we did our final project we were given an opportunity to apply to an internship at Techin.

So, this program helped me grow and implement my knowledge, develop my communication and problem solving skill, get certified and become a Data Scientist.
