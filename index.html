My Favorite Books

Out of all the books I have read this ones are my all favorites.

The Alchemist by Paulo Coelho: I read this book back in my junior year in high school.This book has an interesting concept about finding yourself.

Anna Kerenina by Leo Tolstoy in 1878: This book narrates about a married women who fall in love with another person. It is a fight between temptation and loyality all because of love.

The Perks of Being A Wallflower by Stephen Chbosky: This book is about a teenager trying to process his emotions about his aunt's death and his best friend's suicide.

The Laws of Human Nature by Rodert Greene: This book is the study of human behavior.It helped me understand human psycology and improve myself.
