My Love For Rock

I was introduced to rock music at the age of 13 by my cousin who loves to listen to it. The first song I have ever listened was In the end by Linkin Park which was pretty much famous at that time. She gave me some rock songs and I started to love it. This love developed into my personality. The beautiful base, the drums but most of all the unique voices of the singers is a beautiful harmony that gives me peace. I believe rock music is a beautiful music that anyone can listen to feel uplifted.

Rock music has its own classifications like classic, heavy metal, metallic and it has types like punk rock or gothic rock. But I am a big fan of the classic rocks which are the old 90th and way back. The smooth settled voice flows slowly with the base into my ears and it feels like I am alone in this wide world. It is a form of freedom rebellion, and a sense of calmness. For me, every rock music I listen to have a connection with a bit of my anger, It is sad that rock music listened only by limited people but I deem rock music expresses the deep thoughts everyone thinks about now and then. So, anyone can relate to it.

Sometimes I struggle to study because I want to listen to rock but now I have manage to study without it. If opportunities knock on my door and present me with a chance of learning musical instruments I am sure I will choose the base guitar and the cello. The beauty and deepness of their sound is extraordinary.

To listen to rock people should not feel like a rebel, punk or Goth, it is just a beautiful music that can be related to anyone with any type of lifestyle.
